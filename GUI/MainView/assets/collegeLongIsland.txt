College Long Island
Gin : 2
Tonic : 5
Rum : 2
Whiskey : 2
Coke : 1

Description:
Sometimes called the "Longer Island", this
cocktail will leave you wandering a Denny's
parking lot in Northern Alaska at 3am. With
Gin, Rum, Whiskey, Tonic, and Coke it's a long
island from hell. Enjoy with extreme caution.
